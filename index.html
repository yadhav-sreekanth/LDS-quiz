About Little Daffodils School

Established on 9th May 1990 by our founder Mr. N Ramachandran Nair, Little Daffodils School is a prestigious institution in Calicut (Kerala), affiliated with the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE), Delhi. Since its inception, the school has stood as a beacon of quality education, nurturing young minds with values, knowledge, and discipline. Our commitment has always been to provide not just education, but an environment where character, creativity, and leadership blossom.

About This Website

The Little Daffodils Quiz Club Website is created as a modern digital platform for students to expand their horizons beyond the classroom. It is not merely a quiz space, but a learning companion that sharpens the intellect, stimulates curiosity, and builds confidence in young minds. Here, children engage with knowledge interactively, developing their reasoning, analytical abilities, and awareness of the world around them. By participating in quizzes and learning challenges, students not only enrich their academic foundation but also foster teamwork, healthy competition, and critical thinking — essential qualities for future leaders. This website stands as a bridge between traditional learning and modern knowledge-building, making education exciting, dynamic, and purposeful.

Our Founder

Mr. N Ramachandran Nair, the visionary behind Little Daffodils School, believed in the power of education to shape society. His dedication to nurturing holistic development in children has transformed countless lives. His ideals of love, service, and sacrifice remain the foundation on which this institution continues to thrive.
